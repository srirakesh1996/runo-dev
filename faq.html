Getting to know Runo

Runo is a mobile-first CRM platform that helps businesses manage and track calls made through SIM-based mobile phones. It logs calls automatically, captures dispositions, and provides call recording, reporting, and integrations.

Unlike cloud telephony CRMs that use VoIP, Runo uses your mobile SIM to make and receive calls. It logs calls made directly from the mobile device, ensuring better connectivity and control.

Sales teams, customer support, field executives, real estate, education consultants, NBFCs, and any business that relies heavily on mobile calling.
